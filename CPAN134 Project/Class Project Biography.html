PERSONAL HISTORY

Early Life

Born in Brampton, Canada in 1996, I spent much of my younger years traveling to new places. I'd moved to Rwanda in 1998. My paternal grandmother lived with us while there. Unfortunately, a tragedy eventually resulted in my family moving to Tanzania in 2001. After staying there for a year, my mother got a job in Vienna, Austria and we moved in 2002. I spent the next 8 years living there and learning German. Despite growing accustomed and developing a sense of home, I transferred to a boarding school in British Columbia, Canada in 2009. Upon graduating in 2014, I spent several years at a few universities in a Chemical Engineering program. However, the program didn't end up sitting well with me and I spent a few years working retail in Thunder Bay, Ontario from 2019 to 2022. Upon returning to Brampton, I spent a few more years working odd jobs before deciding to return to school by applying to Humber College for Computer Programming, where I've been studying ever since.
